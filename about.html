Meet the Founder

Anjali Vira

Creator • Lawyer • Storyteller • Founder of Click & Connect Media

Mam's journey proves that when there's purpose, the path always finds you.

Twelve years ago, she was a property lawyer, working with Tina Ambani. That phase taught her discipline, leadership, and strong professional values.

Then motherhood happened. Life slowed down, but her dreams didn't.

During her first pregnancy, she started an Instagram page just to document her motherhood journey without any plans. She simply wanted a space to share her emotions and the real, unfiltered side of becoming a mother. She didn't even know what Content Creation was when she started, and then that honest space slowly became The Sassy Mom.

"She was a lawyer who became a mother. A mother who became a creator. A creator who became a founder."

And above all, a woman who believes that every story — yours included — deserves to be heard.
