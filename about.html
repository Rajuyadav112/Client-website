Our Story

From a personal journey to a thriving community.

Meet the Founder

Apoorva Singh

During her first pregnancy, she started an Instagram page just to document her motherhood journey without any plans. She simply wanted a space to share her emotions and the real, unfiltered side of becoming a mother.

She didn't even know what Content Creation was when she started, and then that honest space slowly became The Sassy Mom.

"A lawyer who became a mother. A mother who became a creator. A creator who became a founder."

The Journey

It grew into more than just a page—it became a space where women openly share their personal journeys, struggles, wins, and emotions without fear of judgment. A platform where real conversations happen, stories are heard, and women feel seen, understood, and supported.

More women started relating, opening up, and sharing their own stories. What started with zero followers grew into a community of 109K+ women. Along the way, she worked for 100+ brands.

But she knew she wanted to build something bigger—something that helped others grow too. That’s where Click & Connect Media was born.

Today, through Click & Connect Media, she helps brands and creators find their voice.
