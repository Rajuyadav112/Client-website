Our Story

Meet the Founder

Anjali Vira

Creator • Lawyer • Storyteller • Founder of Click & Connect Media

Mam's journey proves that when there's purpose, the path always finds you.

Twelve years ago, she was a property lawyer, working with Tina Ambani. That phase taught her discipline, leadership, and strong professional values.

Then motherhood happened. Life slowed down, but her dreams didn't.

During her first pregnancy, she started an Instagram page just to document her motherhood journey without any plans. She simply wanted a space to share her emotions and the real, unfiltered side of becoming a mother. She didn't even know what Content Creation was when she started, and then that honest space slowly became The Sassy Mom.

"She was a lawyer who became a mother. A mother who became a creator. A creator who became a founder."

And above all, a woman who believes that every story — yours included — deserves to be heard.

The Journey

It grew into more than just a page—it became a space where women openly share their personal journeys, struggles, wins, and emotions without fear of judgment. A platform where real conversations happen, stories are heard, and women feel seen, understood, and supported.

More women started relating, opening up, and sharing their own stories. What started with zero followers grew into a community of 109K+ women. Along the way, she worked for 100+ brands and helped many creators and brands find confidence through content creation.

But she knew she wanted to build something bigger—something that helped others grow too. That's where Click & Connect Media was born.

Today, through Click & Connect Media, she helps brands and creators find their voice.

Brands get to clearly show who they are and what they stand for, while creators get a space to share their story, purpose, and vision in an honest way that truly connects with people.
