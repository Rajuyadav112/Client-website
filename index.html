What is SEO and why is it important?

The practice of optimizing your website and content to improve its visibility on search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. It involves a combination of strategies.

SEO is a long-term strategy. While some improvements can be seen in a month, significant results usually take 3-6 months.
SEO focuses on organic traffic (unpaid), while PPC (Pay-Per-Click) involves paying for ads to appear at the top of search results. Both are effective but serve different timelines.
We track key metrics such as organic traffic, keyword rankings, click-through rates (CTR), and conversion rates to measure success.
Our pricing is customized based on your business needs and goals. We offer flexible packages to suit different budgets.
